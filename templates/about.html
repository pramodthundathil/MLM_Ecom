Who We Are

Direct Selling Employess Society

Director Selling Employees Society is registered under Travancore Cochin Literary, Scientific and charitable societies act 12/1955 Reg. No: KLM/TC/87/2024

This organization is formed by a group of democrats who have experience and Personal relationship in the field of direct selling in Kerala and other states of India.

Its governance is to conduct the Social Welfare Activities of the Society on behalf of the members by democratically electing at least seven members of the governing body every five years.

A pannel was constituted from among the mebr mbers of the society and this panel selected the officers of the governing body for the next five years and this was unanimously approved by the general body.

Our Aim and Vision

AIM

The Society works for the social, cultural, educational and economic uplift of the members and their family members who have been working unorganized for years in the field of direct selling.

The Society also aims to help members who are interested in becoming self- entrepreneurs in the field of direct selling and agriculture.

VISION

The main vision of the society is to protect and help the unorganized, destitute and socially isolated individuals and their family memebrs in the field of direct selling and agriculture.

The Society Directly and inclusively engages the members to intitiate various initiative under the Department of Women Impowerment for the educational, cultural and economic growth of women who are family members of the society.

MISSON

The Society is working tirelessly to achieve the goal of eradicating poverty in our country by 2030.
