Terms and Conditions

ADCOS.IN is registered under MSME and has its registered office in Pathanapuram, Kollam District. The company is involved in the business of affiliated marketing (direct selling) of various products and services such as lifestyle items, food and grocery, books and stationery, health and beauty care, electric and electronics products, full value coupons, and vouchers.

ADCOS.IN also provides services in online ticketing (bus, train, flight) and bill payments (electricity, gas, credit card bills). ADCOS.IN offers opportunities to purchase any product of any company, including multinational, branded, and non-branded products.

ADCOS.IN appoints freelance entrepreneurs across the country to introduce our business system and sell our products. Interested customers can purchase products or services and gradually become independent entrepreneurs, providing them with a vast opportunity to grow their business in their own way. ADCOS.IN does not charge any registration fees to become an entrepreneur.

To file the application form, the entrepreneur is advised to go through the terms and conditions mentioned on the official website of the company.

  • Entrepreneur – A customer who consumes a minimum of ₹2000 in business volume becomes an entrepreneur.
